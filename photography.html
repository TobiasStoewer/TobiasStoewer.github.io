Photography

My Story

It was always my passion to create and shape things. I got my first camera when I was 11 years old. It was a small Canon citycam but I didn’t spent too much time using it. This changed when I got my first smart phone which enabled me to always take my camera with me whereever I went.

Some years ago I got a Nikon compact camera and I fell in love with it! The imagequality and all the different settings where overwhelming and I learned a lot using it. But after several years I got to the point where the limited possibilities of the camera frustrated me so I upgraded to my first APSC camera: The Sony Alpha 6000.

This camera became my companion and it still is today. During my time at the university I met Andi Frank who gave a lecture for our course. The tips and tricks he showed us really inspired me and that was the first time I got educated about photography.

Even though I feel like I’ve already developed quite a bit since I got my first camera I always want to keep pushing forward. On the basis of Imogen Cunningham: My best photo will be the one I take tomorrow.
