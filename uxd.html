About UXD

I'm studying User Experience Design at the universtiy of applied sciences Ingolstadt (THI), in Bavaria (Germany).
Usually people don't really understand what UXD is supposed to mean. So for everyone who is confused about what I’m actually doing, here is a quote from Don Norman who is one of the most important people in my field:

‘User experience’ encompasses all aspects of the end-user’s interaction with the company, its services, and its products.

In general it’s about setting the user as the centre of the whole development process. Every desicion is done in the interest of the user and the user decides if something is good or bad. That way every product covers everything the enduser needs in the way it's needed.
For further information check out this article by Don Norman about UX as well as this article about UXD.
